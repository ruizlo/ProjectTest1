Key Concepts

FC Barcelona Femeni:
Currently ranking #1 in the Liga F, FC Barcelona Women's team was founded in 1970. Almost 100 years after the men's team.
Liga F:
The first, and most competitive, women's soccer league in Spain. It is the equivalent of La Liga, the men's Spanish league.
Team Roster 25-26:
Like other sports, soccer seasons begin with the half end of one year and the beginning of another. Within this period, many players have contracts end or they are loaned. Therefore, this squad is smaller than last season's due to these transfers, loans, and contract expirations.
FC Barcelona Innovation:
In recent years, notable figure such as Michelle Kang, have invested in research to optimize performance and health of female athletes. FC Barcelona, although it has been conducting research, has started to pay more attention to how their research findings impact their female athletes.
Championships:
Many of their titles are won by competing against other Spanish teams and winning titles such as Copa de la Reina or the Spanish Super Cup. However, other titles such as the Champion's League come from winning against other qualifying European teams.
