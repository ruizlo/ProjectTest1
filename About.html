About

Project

This project is meant to test out knowledge on using HTML, CSS, and Javascript to develop a website.
Furthermore, we were given the option to choose our own topic, meaning we are allowed to express our creativity while working on this project.

Topic

As someone who would like to work in sports and entertainment, I chose to do my project on my favorite team. FC Barcelona Femeni is one of the best teams in the world and their titles confirm it. However, due to the lack of attention on women's sports as a whole, female athletes do not receive the care and training to reach their fullest potential. Recently, a couple clubs in the U.S. Women's soccer league has started funding research focusing on female athletes. As I complete this project, I am able to research what this specific club is doing to help their world-class athletes improve.
